Your Mental Health Assessment:

Hydration Plan

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Carry a reusable water bottle with you to remind yourself to stay hydrated throughout the day. Water helps to improve energy levels, maintain skin health, and detoxify the body.

Diet Plan

Breakfast: Whole grain oatmeal with berries, chia seeds, and a handful of nuts. A glass of fresh orange juice or herbal tea.

Lunch: Grilled chicken or tofu salad with leafy greens, avocado, and quinoa. Drink water with lemon to stay refreshed.

Dinner: Baked salmon or a vegetarian stir-fry with brown rice and steamed vegetables. Avoid eating too late to improve digestion and sleep quality.

Foods to Include in Your Diet:

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Fruits (apples, bananas, berries)
  • Whole grains (quinoa, brown rice)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, nuts, seeds)
  • Lean proteins (chicken, turkey, beans, tofu)
  • Hydrating foods (cucumbers, watermelon, celery)

Health Precautions

  • Ensure to get 7-9 hours of sleep every night for better physical and mental health.
  • Exercise regularly, aiming for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week.
  • Reduce stress through relaxation techniques such as meditation or deep breathing.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Limit your intake of processed foods, sugars, and unhealthy fats.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and improve skin health.
  • Practice good hygiene, wash your hands regularly, and avoid touching your face.
  • Schedule regular health checkups with your doctor for early detection of potential health issues.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption to lower the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Maintain a positive mental attitude by practicing gratitude and seeking support when needed.
